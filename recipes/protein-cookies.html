Tasty snack thats also high in protein

Healthier alternative to the traditional chocolate chip cookie, with whey protein powder of your choice. 9g of protein per serving!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups whey protein powder
  • 1 cup whole wheat flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teasopoon salt
  • 1 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 6 tablespoons sucralose and brown sugar blend
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 (10oz) bag of chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F
  2. Combine protein powder, whole wheat flour, baking soda, and salt in a bowl
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, brown sugar, and vanilla extract together in a seperate bowl. Add eggs one at a time. Slowly beat in flour mixture. Stir in chocolate chips by hand
  4. Drop tablespoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets
  5. Bake in preheated oven until edges are golden brown, 9-12 minutes. Let cool for about 2 minutes before removing cookies and placing on a cooling rack
