Homemade Soft Pretzels

Back Home

Soft and chewy on the inside, crisp and salty on the outside

Homemade pretzel recipe based on the delicious mall/fair pretzels. They are a bit sweeter than other types, and are buttery

Ingredients

  • Water: 5 - 1/4 cups water, divided
  • Yeast: 4 teaspoons active dry yeast dissolved in water with sugar
  • Sugar: Dissolve a teaspoon of white suhar with the yeast and use 1/2 cup white sugar for the dough
  • Flour: All-purpose flour gives the dough structure
  • Salt: Sprinkle on top of pretzels
  • Oil: Coat a bowl with vegetable oil before you let the dough rise
  • Baking soda: Helps the dough rise

Steps

  1. Activate the yeast with sugar and water
  2. Make the dough and knead it into a ball
  3. Place the dough ball in an oil bowl, turning the ball to coat
  4. Cover and let rise
  5. Turn the dough out, divide into equal pieces, roll into ropes, and shape into pretzels
  6. Dip each pretzel in a mixture of hot water and salt
  7. Sprinkle with salt and bake until brown

Shape pretzels

  1. Roll the dough into long ropes (about 15-18 inches each)
  2. Twist the ends of the dough together
  3. Fold the dough in the opposite direction, over the bottom curve
