Peanut Butter and Banana Smoothie


Refreshing peanut butter banana smoothie. Easy to make!

Servings: 4
Per Serving: 335 calories; protein 12.8g; carbohydrates 34.1g; fat 18.8g; cholesterol 9.8mg; sodium 202.8mg.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups ice cubes

Steps

  1. Place bananas, milk, peanut butter, honey, and ice cubes in a blender; blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
